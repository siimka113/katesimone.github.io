About Me

In my early career, I improved efficiency at a bike repair non profit, and was the founding designer at a fintech startup. I am driven to understand and impact spaces where good design is often overlooked. My biggest strengths are listening and working with uncertainty, which I believe is where the best innovation happens. I'm looking forward to exploring new, interesting problem spaces and developing a deep understanding of them over time.

I'm currently interning at Samsung R&D Canada as a UX Researcher, where I am exploring new product concepts and market opportunities in the health tech space. I'm also a student at Simon Fraser University, pursuing a Bachelor's of Science degree in Interactive Arts and Technology.
